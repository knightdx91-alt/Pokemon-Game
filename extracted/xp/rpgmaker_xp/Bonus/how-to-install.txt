To install bonus content, please click the Materials icon located to the right of the Database icon.

Select Graphics/Characters, Graphics/Tilesets or Graphics/Windowskins. Files that go in each area are in folders with the same name.

Click on "Import" and navigate to where the bonus material is extracted to. Select the file and click "Open". File name will appear on the material list on the right, and will have a red circle in front of it.

To setup the tileset for use in map:
- Select the Database icon.
- Click on the Tilesets tab (3rd from the right).
- Click on "Change Maximum" button at the bottom and increase the number (ex. 51).
- Choose the new entry at the bottom of the list.
- Click on the arrow under "Tileset Graphic" and select the new tileset.
- Fill out the rest of the fields, set the passabilities and click "Ok".
- Right-click on any of the entries in the map tree on the bottom left side of the screen.
- Click on New Map.
- Push the tab under "Tileset" (2nd option from the top) and select your new database tileset.
- Enjoy mapping!
